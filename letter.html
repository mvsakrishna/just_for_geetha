Eunbean,

You locked me in your gaze your eyebrows like gentle bars holding my attention, and your eyes, deep and endless, like the tide, pulling me in without resistance. In that moment, I didn't just look at you I fell through your eyes, past every layer, and sank quietly into the depths of your heart.

Now I find myself lost in the infinite dimensions of your presence, a place where time doesn't exist and emotions speak louder than words. If you ever come looking for me, find me with that same smile the one captured in this picture the smile that unknowingly became my compass and my chaos.

I had let go of poetry long ago, left the pen behind thinking the words had faded forever... but you, just by being you, made the silence stir again. You made the verses return, as if they had only been waiting for someone like you to wake them.

What My Heart Seeks...

I'm searching for a soul who believes in love that starts innocently, without losing the inner child within a love that grows deeper with time and lasts through all stages of life.

Someone who values compassion & emotional connection, who wants to grow old together while building a loving family. Someone who sees love not as a destination, but as a beautiful journey we take hand in hand, day by day, moment by moment.

I'm not sure I've conveyed it properly,
but something like that...
